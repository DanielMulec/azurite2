# Product Language Research Sources

These entries support Azurite's geological product vocabulary. Usage rules and
the entry template live in `docs/research-sources.md`.

### Utah Geological Survey: Azurite And Malachite From The Blue Crystal Mine

- URL: https://geology.utah.gov/map-pub/survey-notes/lisbon-valley-copper-project/
- Accessed: 2026-07-16
- Area: Azurite cluster terminology
- Use when: Explaining the geological grounding for Azurite Cluster.
- Notes: Describes azurite specimens from the Lisbon Valley copper project as
  collectible crystal clusters.
- Caveats: Supports the mineral metaphor. It does not define Azurite's product
  model.

### Geology.com: Azurite

- URL: https://geology.com/minerals/azurite.shtml
- Accessed: 2026-07-16
- Area: Azurite cluster terminology
- Use when: Looking for an accessible specimen example that uses cluster for
  azurite crystals.
- Notes: Describes an azurite specimen as a cluster of blade-shaped crystals.
- Caveats: This is a secondary educational source. Prefer geological surveys or
  mineral references for scientific claims.

### USGS: The Lodes Of The South Tintic Mining District, Utah

- URL: https://pubs.usgs.gov/unnumbered/70047750/report.pdf
- Accessed: 2026-07-16
- Area: Lode and vein terminology
- Use when: Explaining why lode is the product term for a group formed through
  cross-cluster veins.
- Notes: Describes a lode as an assemblage of veins, veinlets, or stringers
  treated as one ore body.
- Caveats: Azurite adapts the geological relationship into product language. It
  does not copy a mining classification into implementation architecture.

### USGS: MAS Deposit Information Manual

- URL: https://pubs.usgs.gov/of/1998/0512/pdf/masnpdictionary.pdf
- Accessed: 2026-07-16
- Area: Vein-network terminology
- Use when: Explaining the geological grounding for networks formed by
  intersecting veins.
- Notes: Defines stockwork using numerous mineralized veins that traverse rock
  and form a network through mutual intersection.
- Caveats: The source supports the relationship metaphor. Azurite Vein has its
  own product-level definition and certainty model.

### Handbook Of Mineralogy: Azurite

- URL: https://www.handbookofmineralogy.org/pdfs/azurite.pdf
- Accessed: 2026-07-16
- Area: Azurite mineral habits
- Use when: Checking mineralogical descriptions of azurite specimens and
  aggregate habits.
- Notes: Documents azurite's mineral properties and aggregate habits.
- Caveats: Mineralogical descriptions provide brand grounding only and do not
  determine product behavior.
